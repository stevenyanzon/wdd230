Website Planning

Target Audience:

Students

This includes pretty much anyone of all ages who is studying face to face who are required to go to campus and those who are studying at home. In today’s world, being updated with things going on is almost already a need specially on how advanced learning is today. We aim to aid these students to be updated and help them work out their schedule based on the weather forecast. There’s a lot of activities in a student’s life that determines whether the forecast would allow it.

Business Owners/Employees

From the CEO down to Staffs, from Fisherman to Farmer etc. This covers everyone who is critical and really have the needs for weather updates. Business owners would want to make sure to have prepare everything that is needed in case of bad storm forecast to protect their business and employees or farmers that will be trying to harvest what ever they can before a bad weather destroys everything. Same with fisherman. They will never risk their lives in a middle of ocean knowing that a storm is coming. These are only example of who this website tries to cater to and provide the service to help them and the general public.

Scenarios

Scenario 1 - What will be the weather forecast today?

Scenario 2 - Is school cancelled for today due to storm coming?

Scenario 3 - When should we schedule our school tour/activities?

Scenario 4 - I have a flight on Wednesday. How’s the weather for that day?

Scenario 5 - Which town will be most affected by the storm?
