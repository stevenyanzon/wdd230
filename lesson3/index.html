Website Planning

Site Name

Weather Bee

Purpose

This website aims to collect as much data as possible and provide weather updates for the towns of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven. With this, citizens will be able to check for themselves and prepare the things they needed depending upon the weather forecast for the day. It aims to help or ease the worries of people and businesses in town with the upcoming uncertainties. With the data collected, this website will forecast weather updates for the next up coming days and will make necessary adjustments when needed.
